### Description
King Power App is Thailand’s leading travel retail shopping application, offering users a seamless and engaging shopping experience across a wide range of premium travel and lifestyle products. The app is engineered with a scalable and modular architecture to ensure maintainability, high performance, and smooth integration with evolving business requirements.

### Tech Stack
- **Clean Architecture** – enforces separation of concerns and maintainable, testable code.
- **MVP (Model–View–Presenter)** – provides a clear contract between the presentation and view layers.
- **Kotlin** – enables concise, expressive, and type-safe Android development.
- **Dagger** – manages dependency injection for modular and efficient component wiring.
- **RxJava** – supports reactive data streams and asynchronous event handling.
- **GraphQL** – optimizes network data fetching and minimizes over-fetching.
- **Epoxy** – simplifies complex RecyclerView implementations with a declarative, component-based approach.
